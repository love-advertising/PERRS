tropical dose
Cruzan Light Rum and Myers's Original Dark Rum provide complex notes of oak and molasses. Tropical citrus fruits of pineapple and orange underline subtle flavors of coconut and golden turmeric, topped off with aromatic nutmeg. A modern take on the classic Painkiller, this refreshing and invigorating combination of rum and turmeric creates a healthy dose of the tropics that transports you with every sip.
