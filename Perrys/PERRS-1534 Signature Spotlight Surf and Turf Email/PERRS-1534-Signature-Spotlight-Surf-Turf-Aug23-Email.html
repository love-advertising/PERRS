The Perry’s Twist on Steakhouse Classics
WHICH WAY DO YOU FILET?
Perry’s Filet Three Ways is an 8 oz. filet, sliced into three medallions,
giving you the choice to find your favorite combination — individually
topped with a 3-Peppercorn au Poivre, Oscar Style, or Coffee Crusted.
3-Peppercorn au Poivre
Perry's classic filet crusted with pink and black peppercorns topped with rich, creamy peppercorn sauce.
Oscar Style
The delicately sweet jumbo lump crabmeat topped with Béarnaise and served with asparagus puts a twist on the expected surf and turf.
Coffee Crusted
We combined our tender filet with a coffee crust to create a smooth, earthly flavor profile.
FEED YOUR INDECISION
Each crowned filet medallion is an exquisite journey into diverse flavors,
showcasing the perfect way for you to have it all. Indulge in the ultimate
dining experience with three uniquely delicious filet medallions.
Join us for a culinary experience sure to leave you satisfied.
