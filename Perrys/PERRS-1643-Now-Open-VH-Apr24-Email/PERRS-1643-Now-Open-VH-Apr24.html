Perry's Steakhouse & Grille has opened its doors in Vernon Hills, Illinois
at Hawthorn Mall, marking its third location in Chicagoland.
From date nights to family dinners to business meetings,
we have all of your favorite award-winning menu items.

Our expansive main dining room provides guests an
opportunity to soak in the Perry's Rare and Well Done® experience,
with glimpses of our chefs in our open kitchen along with a
spectacular wine wall and vibrant Bar 79®, complete with
lively island seating and outdoor dining. Our four beautifully
appointed private dining rooms are the perfect place to transform
your future milestones into cherished memories.

We can’t wait to serve you!
