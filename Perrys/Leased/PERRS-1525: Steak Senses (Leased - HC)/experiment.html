STEAK LOVERS,
COME TO YOUR SENSES.
 
At Perry's, we know steaks. Since 1979, we have stayed true to our butcher shop roots with
4 decades of Prime USDA-aged steaks.
 
INDULGE YOUR SENSES
 
Sight On viewing the perfect steak, you will notice a ring of crust forming around the edges and moving up or down the side of the steak. This is formed with minimal movement during the cooking process.
 
Did You Know: Perry's Steaks are prepared in an infrared broiler at 1800 degrees and topped with Perry's Signature Steak Seasoning and Signature Steak Butter for the perfect caramelization.
Sound The ultra-satisfying sizzle of fresh-from-the-flame steaks lets you know the crust is forming.
 
Did You Know: At Perry's, serving steaks on a hot iron plate allows the sear to continue to the table so you can enjoy this sound.
 
Smell That tantalizing aroma wafting toward your table. It's the smoky, savory stuff a nose dreams about. Some describe a slight bleu cheese fragrance resulting from the lactic acid being released during the aging process.
 
Did You Know: Perry's Steaks are aged 28 days for PRIME cuts. All Perry's Steaks are cut-fresh daily to ensure our customers a tender bite.
Taste Ah, the mighty mouth. The flavor that makes your tastebuds do a happy dance. This comes from an expert affinity to hand-select the highest quality steak cuts.
 
Did You Know: Perry's offers premium steak options such as True Japanese A-5 Wagyu Beef, Texas Kobe-style Wagyu Beef, USDA-aged PRIME Angus, and Certified Upper Choice Beef that provide an incredibly flavorful steak.
 
Touch It makes perfect sense: A gloved touch or two helps determine the appropriate temperature. A steak that's soft to the touch is designated rare, while a medium rare steak feels a bit springy. A well-done steak is firm to the touch, but we don't recommend this preparation method as it affects the integrity of the cut.
 
Did You Know: The quality of your steak knife should match the quality of your steak. The perfect steak is elevated with a great steak knife.
